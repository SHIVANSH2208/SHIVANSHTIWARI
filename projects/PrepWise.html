PrepWise

  • Tech Stack: TypeScript, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
  • Project Link: PrepWise
  • Certificate URL: HackIndia

AI-powered mock interviews Prepwise uses artificial intelligence to conduct realistic mock interviews, helping candidates practice HR, technical, and behavioral questions.

Instant feedback & analysis After each interview, it provides detailed feedback on communication skills, confidence, clarity, filler words, pace, and overall performance.

Role-based interview preparation It offers interview practice tailored to specific job roles, domains, and experience levels (freshers to experienced professionals).

Improves communication & confidence By repeatedly practicing in an interview-like environment, users gain confidence, better articulation, and structured answering skills.

Useful for placement & job interviews Prepwise is widely used by students and job seekers preparing for campus placements, IT companies, and corporate interviews.
